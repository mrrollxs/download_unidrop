How to Join the Beta

Android: It's a two-step process. First, join our official Google Group. Then, use the second link to opt-in and download the app from the Play Store. It may take a few minutes for access to be granted.

iPhone, iPad & Mac: Our Apple beta is distributed via TestFlight. You will need to install Apple's TestFlight app from the App Store on your device before you can install UniDrop. The same link works for iOS and macOS.

Windows: Simply click the link to go to a private Microsoft Store page and install the app directly.
